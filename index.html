Welcome

Ripple is an open source P2P payment system.

Ripple lets you easily, cheaply, and safely send money over the Internet to anyone, anywhere in the world. Because Ripple is P2P software, no individual, corporation, or government controls Ripple.

Anyone can create a Ripple account. Ripple accounts can not be shutdown by any corporation or government. The Ripple network operates through the combined efforts of the copies of the Ripple software that are running all over the world.
