Welcome

Newcoin is an open source P2P payment system.

Newcoin lets you easily, cheaply, and safely send money over the Internet to anyone, anywhere in the world. Because Newcoin is P2P software, no individual, corporation, or government controls Newcoin.

Anyone can create a Newcoin account. Newcoin accounts can not be shutdown by any corporation or government. The Newcoin network operates through the combined efforts of the copies of the Newcoin software that are running all over the world.
